Optional notes the LLM uses **only** when naming transactions and spending-mix bars. Treat this as *your* ground truth: merchant substrings, typical amounts, and what a charge **is** (rent, daycare, gym, etc.).

**How to write facts**

- One idea per bullet; short phrases are fine.
- Include whatever disambiguates: payee text fragment, rough amount, cadence, city.
- Do not put full account numbers, card PANs, or secrets here—use the same level of detail you’d put in a private note.
