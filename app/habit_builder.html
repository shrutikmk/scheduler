    How the habit program works

    Phase 1 uses seven Sunday–Saturday calendar weeks. Week 1 is the week that contains your program start; week k needs k logged days anywhere that week (only on/after your start date counts). After Phase 1, two Phase 2 models run in parallel: Progress (accent bar) milestones 8→90 can be “packed” into your latest contiguous streak; any 90-day streak in Phase 2 completes the program on that path. Long run (green bar) is the original ladder with required rest days between streaks — same rules the day planner deadlines use for streak requirements. Overall complete when either path reaches 100%. 100% points = 4095 total. Your habits are written to IndexedDB after every change and mirrored to localStorage for recovery. Data stay on this device only.
